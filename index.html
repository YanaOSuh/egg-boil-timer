Egg Boil TIMER

1. Select how you want your eggs cooked

6 min

Runny yolk

(quite soft with a liquidy yolk, the white fairly cooked)

8 min

Soft

(the yolk is soft, but it's not liquid, slightly jammy)

10 min

Medium

(there's just a smidge of softness left in the yolk)

12 min

Semi-hard

(egg is a bit firmer, with a lighter yolk)

14 min

Hard

(egg with the lightest yolk and a firm white)

2. Bring a pot of water to a boil with enough water to cover the eggs. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, place eggs in the water. Then, immediately turn the heat back up to a boil.

3. Start the timer by clicking on "Start timer"
When the timer is done, it will ring

00 : 00

Your perfect EGGS are READY

4. Take the eggs out of the boiling water and put them in cold water to cool down
